I am a contemporary designer working at the intersection of art, media and technology. Through my research-based practice I frequently investigate and posit questions relating to the political, the ethical and the aesthetic.
My work often tackles contemporary issues plaguing society and serves as a medium through which injustices can be fought, by shedding light on marginalised communities as well as exposing corruption and discrimination.

I grew up in Malta, a tiny island in the Mediterranean and moved to Scotland 5 years ago to study where I now work.
I love it here and am absolutely in love with the Highlands.

I'm taking this course because:

  • I would love to career switch to a tech role
  • I've always been fascinated with what code can do
  • As a designer I'm interested in the creative applications of code

3 reasons I love Scotland:

  1. The Highlands
  2. The Whisky & Gin
  3. Harry Potter
